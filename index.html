WHO WE ARE

We're a student run CS/AI/ML lab, a small technical community exploring the frontier of models. We run weekly hack sessions every Saturday 1-7pm at CS deparment labs, all programmers are welcome! The sessions are chill and come and go as you please (though we do hope you'll stay for all of it). Ping us on discord when you arrive and we'll come get you.

If you're interested in speaking at one of our events, email us.

WHO WE WANT

Student researchers who want built-in structure for their research. Builds and hackers who want to create.

If you're not pushing boundaries, you're wasting your time.

The society is run by Eric and friends.
